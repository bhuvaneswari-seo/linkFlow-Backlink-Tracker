About lost link detection — Browsers cannot automatically ping URLs due to security restrictions (CORS). Instead, linkFlow flags links as Check after 30 days without a verify action. Click Verify on any row to open the page, then confirm it is live or mark it as lost.

What linkFlow does

A complete backlink tracking system. Add every link you build, track its status, monitor DR health, and manage lost link recovery. Data saves in your browser and persists between sessions on the same device.

Getting started

1
Go to Link tracker and click + Add new link every time a link goes live — or use Import from Ahrefs / Semrush to bulk-import from a CSV export.
2
Fill in domain, anchor text, link type, DR, status, and month — these fields power all charts and KPIs.
3
Overview auto-updates with KPI cards and charts the moment you save a link.
4
Links not verified in 30 days are flagged as Check — a red alert bar appears at the top of the tracker.
5
Click Verify on any row → page opens → click Still Live or Mark Lost to update status and reset the 30-day clock.
6
Lost links flow into Lost & recovery automatically — assign a re-outreach action immediately.
7
Use Export CSV (top right) to back up your data or share with a client.

Field reference

Linking domain *
Root domain linking to you — e.g. ahrefs.com
Anchor text *
The clickable text of your backlink
Page with link
Full URL of the page where your link appears
Your target URL
The page on your site being linked to
Link type *
Guest Post / Resource Page / Broken Link / Niche Edit / Digital PR / Citation
Status *
Live = active · Lost = removed · Check = needs manual verification
Domain Rating
DR from Ahrefs or DA from Moz (0–100). Powers the DR health tab.
Traffic estimate
Monthly organic traffic of the linking site — e.g. 150K or 2.1M
Dofollow?
Yes = passes link equity · No = nofollow · TBD = not yet confirmed
Month
Reporting month — groups links in the Monthly tab charts
Last checked
Auto-updated when you click Verify. Links not checked in 30+ days are flagged.

Tips

Daily habit
Check the tracker every morning. Red alert bar = links that need verifying today.
Verify workflow
Click Verify → page opens → check the link is still there → come back and click Still Live or Mark Lost.
Lost links
Act within 48 hours. Go to Lost & recovery and assign a re-outreach action immediately.
Export
Use Export CSV anytime to back up your data or share with a client or manager.
Data storage
Saved in your browser's localStorage. Persists between sessions on the same device and browser.
